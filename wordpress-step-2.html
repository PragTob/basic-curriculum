h2>Step 2: Create Attendee scaffold We are using Rails’ scaffolds to generate a starting point that allows us to list, add, remove, edit, and view things; in our case attendees. Coach: What is a scaffold (1. the command, 2. the model name and related db table; pluralization/naming conventions, 3. attributes and types)? What are migrations and when do you need to do them, why? 
rails generate scaffold attendee name:string twitter_handle:string bio:text address:text picture:string
rake db:migrate
rails s
Open http://localhost:3000/attendees in browser. CTRL-C exits the server when you have clicked around for a little.
